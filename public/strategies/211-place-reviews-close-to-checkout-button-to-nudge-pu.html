Place Reviews Close to Checkout Button to Nudge Purchase

Category: Reviews & Reputation

Metadata

  • Type: Reviews & Reputation
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Time to Impact: 1-3 Months
  • Required Tools: None specified

Quick Summary

Include select, high-quality reviews near or just above the final purchase CTA/button on the checkout page, focusing on reviews that address key objections.
-

Full Strategy Details

  1. Place Reviews Close to Checkout Button to Nudge Purchase
  • What It Is: Include select, high-quality reviews near or just above the final purchase CTA/button on the checkout page, focusing on reviews that address key objections.
    • Why It Works: Just before commitment, buyers benefit from reassurance; reviews addressing shipping, product quality, or service ease last “push” conversion by addressing last-minute doubts.
    • When To Use: Ecommerce checkout or product pages with reviews; SaaS may adapt with testimonials near subscription sign-up.
    • Execution Steps:
      1. Select 3-5 reviews that cover common objections (e.g., fast shipping, product effectiveness, customer service).
      2. Place reviews directly above or beside the purchase button or summary box.
      3. Keep review excerpts brief and scannable.
      4. Test different review selections and placements for best lift.
    • Pitfalls & Limits: Overloading checkout area with too many reviews can distract. Low-quality or outdated reviews can hurt trust.
    • Example: Commonly tested approach; reviews about shipping, product, and service shown near checkout nudged completion rates up.
