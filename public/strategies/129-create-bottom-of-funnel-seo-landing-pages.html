Full Strategy Details

Create Bottom of Funnel SEO Landing Pages

What It Is: Developing landing pages specifically tailored for bottom-of-funnel search intent, where users are ready to convert or take a specific action.
Why It Works: Bottom-of-funnel content directly addresses transactional intent, resulting in higher conversion rates, better user engagement, and less susceptibility to AI overview takeover which is more common with informational queries.
When To Use: Ecommerce sites, lead generation, service providers, any business looking to maximize conversions from search traffic.
Execution Steps:

  1. Identify bottom-of-funnel keywords indicating high intent (purchase, sign-up, contact).
  2. Create concise, persuasive landing pages optimized for those keywords.
  3. Include clear calls to action immediately visible (“above the fold”).
  4. Ensure the content directly satisfies the user’s search intent in a straightforward manner.
  5. Drive focused external marketing to these pages to support natural backlink profiles.
    Pitfalls & Limits: Too narrow focus may limit brand awareness potential. Landing pages without sufficient external signals can struggle to rank.
    Example: The podcast author strongly recommends continuing to develop bottom-of-funnel SEO landing pages post-update for durability and consistency.
