Write Original, Useful Content for People, Not Just for Search Engines

Category: Content Strategy & Keywords

Metadata

  • Type: Content Strategy & Keywords
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Time to Impact: 1-3 Months
  • Required Tools: None specified

Quick Summary

Create genuinely helpful, readable content tailored to the real needs of your target audience rather than simply stuffing keywords or relying on AI-generated filler text.

Full Strategy Details

  1. Write Original, Useful Content for People, Not Just for Search Engines
    What It Is: Create genuinely helpful, readable content tailored to the real needs of your target audience rather than simply stuffing keywords or relying on AI-generated filler text.
    Why It Works: Google’s algorithm penalizes low-quality, keyword-stuffed content and values user satisfaction signals (dwell time, lower bounce rate). Content that genuinely helps users ranks better and sustains traffic.
    When To Use: Always. Especially important for competitive topics, medium and large content sites, and when recovering from traffic drops related to Google quality updates.
    Execution Steps:
    1. Identify your searcher persona and their intent.
    2. Research and write detailed content that answers user questions comprehensively and naturally.
    3. Use keywords strategically but avoid overuse or unnatural phrasing.
    4. Avoid copying large blocks of AI-generated text without human editing and improvement.
    5. Continually update content based on user feedback and data.
      Pitfalls & Limits:
    • Blindly using AI-generated content without edits can cause penalization.
    • Keyword stuffing or writing overly promotional text harms UX and rankings.
      Example: A site owner’s pages lost rankings after using ChatGPT-generated SEO copy; recovery started only after rewriting content manually with readers in mind.
