Tracking and Maintaining Review Recency for Google Business Profiles

What It Is: Regularly acquiring new reviews to keep your Google Business Profile’s review count fresh and competitive compared to local competitors.
Why It Works: Google factors “review recency” into local ranking algorithms; stale review profiles lose local pack rankings over time.
When To Use: Local businesses competing in Google Local Pack listings.
Execution Steps:

  • Benchmark competitors’ monthly new review volume.
  • Set a monthly review acquisition goal equal to or exceeding competitor averages.
  • Incentivize employees to ask customers for reviews (no incentives to customers).
  • Monitor reviews monthly and adjust efforts accordingly.
    Pitfalls & Limits: Incentivizing customers for reviews violates Google guidelines and legal regulations; avoid this. Reviews must be authentic.
    Example: Clients losing ranking after they stopped getting new reviews; internal tools show competitors getting 10 reviews/month, client only gets 2 — need to increase to stay competitive.
