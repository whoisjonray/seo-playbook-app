Tracking and Maintaining Review Recency for Google Business Profiles

What It Is

Regularly acquiring new reviews to keep your Google Business Profile’s review count fresh and competitive compared to local competitors.

Why It Works

Google factors “review recency” into local ranking algorithms; stale review profiles lose local pack rankings over time.

When To Use

Local businesses competing in Google Local Pack listings.

Execution Steps

  • Benchmark competitors’ monthly new review volume.
  • Set a monthly review acquisition goal equal to or exceeding competitor averages.
  • Incentivize employees to ask customers for reviews (no incentives to customers).
  • Monitor reviews monthly and adjust efforts accordingly.

Pitfalls & Limits

Incentivizing customers for reviews violates Google guidelines and legal regulations; avoid this. Reviews must be authentic.
