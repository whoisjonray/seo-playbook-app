Incorporate NoFollow Links in Backlink Profiles

Category: Link Building & Backlinks

Metadata

  • Type: Link Building & Backlinks
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Time to Impact: 1-3 Months
  • Required Tools: None specified

Quick Summary

Actively acquiring nofollow backlinks to create a natural and diverse backlink profile.

Full Strategy Details

  1. Incorporate NoFollow Links in Backlink Profiles
    What It Is: Actively acquiring nofollow backlinks to create a natural and diverse backlink profile.
    Why It Works: Google uses backlink data including nofollow links. A profile with only dofollow links appears manipulative. Nofollow links contribute to natural link diversity, which is favored by search algorithms.
    When To Use: All types of SEO, especially when building or auditing backlink profiles to avoid unnatural link patterns.
    Execution Steps:
  • Don’t avoid nofollow links; seek them naturally through guest posts, social profiles, Q&A sites, etc.
  • Analyze your backlink profile to maintain a balanced mix of dofollow and nofollow links.
  • Avoid exact-match anchor text on all links to prevent spam signals.
    Pitfalls & Limits: Building only nofollow links won’t significantly boost rankings. Ignoring nofollow links altogether may signal unnatural linking.
    Example: Survey shows nearly 80% of SEO pros believe nofollow links affect rankings and are part of a natural backlink profile.
