Full Strategy Details

Create Explicit, Machine-Readable Brand Information Pages for AI

-

What It Is

Publish dedicated pages on your site that provide comprehensive, structured brand information in machine-readable formats (e.g., Markdown files linked in footer or detailed “About Us” pages) to improve AI retrieval and quoting accuracy.

Why It Works

AI models pull from easily accessible, clear, and authoritative brand content; providing a “source of truth” improves accuracy and the likelihood that AI tools will cite your brand correctly in responses.

When To Use

Useful for SaaS, service brands, and companies wanting to control AI-derived brand messaging and for new companies building brand reputation with AI.

Execution Steps

 1. Create a highly detailed brand information page with facts, specialties, testimonials, and history presented clearly.  
 2. Use machine-readable formatting like markdown or structured data (where appropriate).  
 3. Link this page prominently but unobtrusively in your site footer or navigation.  
 4. Test LLM responses by querying them about your brand to check if content is cited.  
 5. Iterate and update the page periodically with new information or to improve clarity.

-

Pitfalls & Limits

 - Too hidden or hard to find pages may not be crawled.  
 - Using overly technical formats that AI does not use could reduce effectiveness.  
 - Duplicate or inconsistent brand messaging across the web can confuse AI.

-

Implementation Checklist

  1. Create a highly detailed brand information page with facts, specialties, testimonials, and history presented clearly.
  2. Use machine readable formatting like markdown or structured data (where appropriate). Link this page prominently but unobtrusively in your site footer or navigation.
  3. Test LLM responses by querying them about your brand to check if content is cited.
  4. Iterate and update the page periodically with new information or to improve clarity.
