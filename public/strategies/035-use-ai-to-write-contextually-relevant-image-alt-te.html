Use AI to Write Contextually Relevant Image Alt Text

Category: AI & Featured Snippets

Metadata

  • Type: AI & Featured Snippets
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Time to Impact: 1-3 Months
  • Required Tools: None specified

Quick Summary

Leverage AI tools (like Google Gemini) to generate image alt text based on the context of the page content rather than keyword-stuffed or generic descriptions.

Full Strategy Details

  1. Use AI to Write Contextually Relevant Image Alt Text
    What It Is: Leverage AI tools (like Google Gemini) to generate image alt text based on the context of the page content rather than keyword-stuffed or generic descriptions.
    Why It Works: Google image search values alt text that is descriptive and contextually tied to the page content, improving image rankings and organic visibility. AI-generated alt text avoids spammy keyword stuffing and captures nuanced meaning.
    When To Use: Ecommerce product pages, service pages, and any website relying on image SEO.
    Execution Steps:
    1. Complete the page content first and have it ready.
    2. Upload the image along with the page content to the AI tool.
    3. Instruct the AI to generate alt text that is contextually relevant to the page content without inserting target keywords explicitly.
    4. Insert the AI-generated alt text on the image in your CMS.
    5. Test and monitor image search traffic for improvement.
      Pitfalls & Limits:
    • Giving AI the target keyword as an input can lead to artificial keyword stuffing.
    • Generic alt text or unrelated descriptions reduce image SEO value.
      Example: Testing across 12 Australian ecommerce stores showed “massive improvements” in rankings after AI-generated contextual alt text updates.
