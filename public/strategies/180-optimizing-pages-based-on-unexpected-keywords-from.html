    • When To Use: Existing content optimization, growing niche sites, or post-launch content refinement.
    • Execution Steps:
      1. Open Google Search Console and check "Performance" for queries related to each page.
      2. Identify valuable keywords you didn‚Äôt anticipate but your page ranks for.
