Full Strategy Details

  1. Local Citation Building via Quality Directories
  • What It Is: Acquiring business listings (‚Äúcitations‚Äù) on reputable local and niche directories relevant to your industry to improve local SEO signals and rankings.
    • Why It Works: Improves local relevance and trust signals for Google My Business and local pack rankings, especially for service-based businesses.
    • When To Use: Local SEO, especially for small businesses like plumbers, local services, and franchises.
    • Execution Steps:
      1. Identify high-quality, niche-specific and general local directories relevant to your business location and industry.
      2. Create or claim business profiles with consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) information.
      3. Prioritize industry-specific directories (e.g., plumbing directories).
      4. Use affordable services or freelancers cautiously (e.g., Fiverr citations) ensuring quality and consistency.
      5. Monitor indexed citations and local search rankings.
    • Pitfalls & Limits: Low-quality citations or inconsistent NAP info can hurt SEO; mass low-quality directory spam can be harmful; citation building is less effective for non-local or non-service e-commerce sites.
    • Example: A Belgian plumber client improved his local ranking to #1 in a large area primarily through directory citations combined with minor on-site improvements.
