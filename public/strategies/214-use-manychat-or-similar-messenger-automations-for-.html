Use ManyChat or Similar Messenger Automations for List Building

Category: Social Media & PR

Metadata

  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Time to Impact: Hours to Days
  • Required Tools: None specified

Quick Summary

Full Strategy Details

Use ManyChat or Similar Messenger Automations for List Building

  • What It Is: Use social media chatbot tools (e.g., ManyChat) to automate lead capture, qualification, and subscription to newsletters via keyword triggers like “DM ‘growth’”.
    • Why It Works: Automates lead funnel initiation, removing manual friction, converts social engagement into direct marketing contacts, often yielding high open and engagement rates.
    • When To Use: Businesses active on Instagram, Facebook, or similar platforms with engaged followers seeking to grow newsletters or lists.
    • Execution Steps:
      1. Set up ManyChat (or equivalent) on social channels connected to your business.
      2. Create a flow triggered by comments with specific keywords that sends relevant offers or guides.
      3. Build in qualification questions for list segmentation and improve targeting.
      4. Decide whether to send immediate welcome emails or allow direct newsletter enrollment.
      5. Track open rates, unsubscribes, and engagement to optimize lead magnets.
    • Pitfalls & Limits: Risk of automatic subscriptions without clear opt-in reduces long-term engagement; must be transparent and compliant with spam laws.
    • Example: Friend grew newsletter from zero to 880,000 in 7-8 months using ManyChat automation.
