    When To Use: All SEO contexts, especially new content creation and content refresh for informational topics.
    Execution Steps:
  2. Identify the keyword or query to target.
  3. Spend time analyzing the searcher‚Äôs intent: Are they looking for how-to info, products, answers, comparisons?

  5. Use user-friendly formatting to keep visitors engaged.
