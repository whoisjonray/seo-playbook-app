Full Strategy Details

Leverage Social Media Platforms (TikTok/Instagram) for Topical Visibility Before Investing in SEO

What It Is: Use social platforms like TikTok or Instagram to gain topical visibility and attract early customers before building organic search authority via SEO.
Why It Works: Social content can provide quicker engagement, brand awareness, and leads, especially for local or niche businesses where Google results may be dominated by ads or large competitors.
When To Use: Startups, local businesses, service providers in competitive niches where SEO is slow or costly.
Execution Steps:
1. Identify where your target audience spends time socially.
2. Create engaging, informative videos/content showcasing your products or services.
3. Build follower base and encourage direct leads via social channels.
4. Use social success and revenue to justify longer-term SEO investments.
5. Later, expand to website SEO with stronger topical authority once business is stable.
Pitfalls & Limits:
- Social platforms’ algorithm changes can reduce visibility unpredictably.
- Over reliance on one channel limits diversified traffic sources.
Example: Pool services and accounting firms getting leads from TikTok and Instagram videos before moving to SEO.
