Creating Unique, Detailed Location Pages for Multi-Location SEO

Quick Summary

Full Strategy Details

  1. Creating Unique, Detailed Location Pages for Multi-Location SEO
    What It Is: Developing separate pages for each location or service area with unique content including genuine reviews, photos, job details, and local cultural references to avoid doorway page penalties.
    Why It Works: Google penalizes thin/page duplication; unique, detailed pages provide differentiated user value and local relevance signals.
    When To Use: Businesses targeting multiple cities or regions, especially local SEO for service providers.
    Execution Steps:
    • List all business locations in footer and/or header with addresses and phone numbers.
    • Create individual pages for each key location including:
      • Full address and hours
      • Embedded map (Google Business Profile)
      • Genuine local reviews and case studies of work at that location
      • Photos of completed jobs at that location
      • Descriptions including local cultural or geographic references
    • Avoid duplicated template content with only minor word swaps.
    • Start small (1-2 pages), test indexing and ranking, and then scale based on success.
      Pitfalls & Limits:
    • Too many pages (>20) without scale or offline signals invite penalties.
    • Pages must not be thin or doorway; superficial changes are insufficient.
    • Highly competitive industries (like lawyers) may struggle with generic location pages.
      Example: Client with 200+ AI-generated unique location pages failed to get most indexed and earned manual penalties despite quality appearance.
