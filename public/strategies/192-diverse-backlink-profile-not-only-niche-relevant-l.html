Full Strategy Details

Diverse Backlink Profile, Not Only Niche-Relevant Links

What It Is: Acquiring backlinks from a variety of websites, not strictly from the same niche, can benefit SEO.
Why It Works: Google values natural backlink profiles that include links from a range of unrelated but authoritative sites, signaling broad trustworthiness rather than manipulative link building.
When To Use: Link building for most sites, especially those not in hyper-niche industries; relevant for both ecommerce and content sites.
Execution Steps:

  • Build relationships and earn links from authoritative sites inside and outside your niche.
  • Maintain a natural backlink profile by avoiding only niche-specific link targets.
  • Monitor your backlink profile for diversity using SEO tools.
    Pitfalls & Limits: Completely irrelevant spammy links can harm; disregard for link quality can be detrimental; niche relevance remains important but not exclusive.
    Example: Website Catalyst observed their sites ranking improvements even when outbound/inbound links were from unrelated sites like golf or lawn care.
