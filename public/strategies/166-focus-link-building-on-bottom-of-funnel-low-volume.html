Focus Link Building on Bottom-of-Funnel, Low-Volume Keywords

Focus Link Building on Bottom-of-Funnel, Low-Volume Keywords

Category: Link Building & Backlinks

Metadata

  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Time to Impact: Hours to Days
  • Required Tools: None specified

Quick Summary

Prioritizing rank efforts on bottom-funnel, lower search volume keywords which are commonly undertargeted but more conversion-prone and easier to rank for without many backlinks.

Full Strategy Details

  1. Focus Link Building on Bottom-of-Funnel, Low-Volume Keywords
    What It Is: Prioritizing rank efforts on bottom-funnel, lower search volume keywords which are commonly undertargeted but more conversion-prone and easier to rank for without many backlinks.
    Why It Works: These keywords have less competition and higher purchase intent; Google rewards helpful content targeting these gaps with higher rankings and better traffic quality.
    When To Use: Smaller sites, ecommerce, local businesses, or marketers looking for quick wins and efficient use of SEO budget.
    Execution Steps:
  • Use keyword research tools to find lower-volume, bottom-funnel keywords.
  • Create tailored content fulfilling specific user needs or transactional queries.
  • Optimize on-page factors thoroughly.
  • Build minimal or no backlinks and monitor ranking progress.
    Pitfalls & Limits: Won't capture large volume traffic; less effective for brand awareness campaigns. May require multiple pages to scale traffic.
    Example: Survey respondents note that many lucrative keywords at the bottom of the funnel are undertargeted, making link building less necessary for ranking.
