Price Testing & Shipping Messaging: Say “We Pay Shipping” Instead of “Free Shipping”

-

Full Strategy Details

  1. Price Testing & Shipping Messaging: Say “We Pay Shipping” Instead of “Free Shipping”
  • What It Is: Instead of “Free Shipping,” use messaging like “We pay shipping” to create a perception of cost absorption and reciprocity, improving customer sentiment and reducing cart abandonment.
    • Why It Works: “Free” implies zero cost which customers know is unrealistic; “We pay shipping” suggests the seller is taking responsibility, triggering a psychological reciprocity effect.
    • When To Use: Ecommerce stores where shipping cost is a major reason for cart abandonment.
    • Execution Steps:
      1. Change banners, messaging, and product page wording from “Free Shipping” to “We pay shipping.”
      2. Bake shipping cost into product price as needed for profitability.
      3. Adjust customer support scripts to reflect seller-paid shipping on returns or exchanges.
      4. Track abandonment and post-purchase feedback on perceived value.
    • Pitfalls & Limits: Not a fix for excessive shipping fees; premium customers might be sensitive to product price increases.
    • Example: Site noticed improved customer support interactions and fewer shipping disputes after changing to “We pay shipping” phrasing.
