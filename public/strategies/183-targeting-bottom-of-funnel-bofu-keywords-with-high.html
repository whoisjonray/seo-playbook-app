Full Strategy Details

Targeting Bottom-of-Funnel (BOFU) Keywords with High Conversion Potential

-

What It Is

Prioritize targeting keywords that indicate purchase intent (BOFU keywords), which typically have lower search volume but higher conversion rates.

Why It Works

Unlike popular informational keywords, BOFU keywords attract users closer to making a purchase, thus yielding higher ROI and revenue despite lower volume.

When To Use

Ecommerce, lead generation sites, service-oriented businesses focusing on revenue and conversions.

Execution Steps

 1. Identify keywords with buyer intent (e.g., “buy,” “services near me,” “best [product]”).  
 2. Use tools or competitor analysis to find BOFU keywords competitors rank for.  
 3. Create or optimize targeted content focused on these keywords’ intent.  
 4. Enhance pages with conversion-focused elements (CTAs, clear offers).

-

Pitfalls & Limits

Lower volume means less overall traffic, so BOFU keywords should be part of a balanced funnel strategy; not suitable if brand awareness is a primary goal.

Implementation Checklist

  1. Identify keywords with buyer intent (e.g., “buy,” “services near me,” “best [product]”).
  2. Use tools or competitor analysis to find BOFU keywords competitors rank for. Create or optimize targeted content focused on these keywords’ intent.
  3. Enhance pages with conversion focused elements (CTAs, clear offers).
