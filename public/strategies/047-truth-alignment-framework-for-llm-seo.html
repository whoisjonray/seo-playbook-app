    • Why It Works: LLMs serve as intermediaries in search for many users, especially for detailed queries comparing brands or products.     • When To Use: Particularly effective for B2B SaaS, enterprise brands, and businesses with complex offerings where users perform detailed research via AI assistants. Also valuable for new or lesser-known brands trying to build trust in AI-based search contexts.
    • Execution Steps:
      1. Collect the key skeptical or deal breaker questions your ICP would ask about your brand or products.
      2. Document the ideal or truthful answers that your top experts or salespeople would give.

      4. Identify content pages influencing incorrect or incomplete AI responses.
      5. Update or create highly retrieval-friendly content on your site that addresses these questions clearly and accurately.
      6. Re-test periodically to measure if AI answers improve in alignment with your content updates.
