Full Strategy Details

Use Press Releases for Earned Media, Not Link Building

What It Is: Distributing newsworthy press releases via recognized wire services to potentially gain editorial coverage and earned links, rather than expecting direct SEO benefit from the press release links themselves.
Why It Works: Google discounts links directly from press release sites, but coverage by legitimate publishers and media outlets can bring authoritative backlinks and brand exposure.
When To Use: Businesses in niches that attract media interest and newsworthy events; companies launching products, reaching milestones, or participating in notable events.
Execution Steps:
1. Write an authentic, newsworthy press release focused on real business developments.
2. Distribute via reputable wire services (e.g., Business Wire, PR Newswire).
3. Monitor for pickup by journalists and media outlets.
4. Leverage any earned editorial links or mentions for SEO and PR campaigns.
Pitfalls & Limits: Press releases themselves rarely cause ranking boosts; using them solely as link building spam is ineffective; success depends on genuine news value and media interest.
Example: Nick confirms that press release links themselves are not ranking factors, but actual earned media coverage can have minor boosts and credibility benefits.
