Google Merchant Center Feed Optimization and Image Use for Ecommerce

What It Is: Correctly manage product feeds in Google Merchant Center with accurate product structuring and use high-quality, relevant images on product pages.
Why It Works: Invalid or poorly structured feeds prevent Google Shopping from properly indexing products, limiting visibility; proper images aid user decision-making and improve click-through and conversion.
When To Use: Ecommerce sites selling physical products appearing in Google Shopping and organic search product listings.
Execution Steps:
1. Audit and validate product feeds for accuracy and standards compliance.
2. Use clear categorization and distinct product variants in the feed.
3. On product pages, use actual product images or screenshots instead of generic stock photos.
4. Optimize images for SEO (alt text, file names, size).
5. Implement CRO techniques to showcase images effectively and improve customer trust.
Pitfalls & Limits:
- Invalid feeds lead to disapproval or poor product listing performance.
- Poor quality or generic images hurt conversion even if indexed.
Example: Many merchants fail proper feed setup or image selection, losing potential traffic and sales.
