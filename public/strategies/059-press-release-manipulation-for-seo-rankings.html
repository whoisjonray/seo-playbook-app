Full Strategy Details

Press Release Manipulation for SEO Rankings

What It Is: Publishing press releases through reputable distribution services to push down undesired content and enhance brand visibility in Google search results.
Why It Works: Press releases distributed on trusted platforms like Associated Press are indexed rapidly by Google and can rank high, often on first page results. They carry authoritative signals and can outrank competitive or negative content due to freshness, relevance, and domain authority of news sites.
When To Use: Effective for reputation management, competitive SEO, crisis management, and brand awareness campaigns. Works well for established brands, startups launching new tools, or businesses facing negative search results.
Execution Steps:

  1. Craft a clear and positive press release focusing on your brand’s key messages or product launches.
  2. Use established press release distribution services such as AB Newswire or others with AP News affiliation.
  3. Consider subscription plans (e.g., $500/year on AB Newswire) for scalable distribution cost benefits.
  4. Publish the press release, ensuring inclusion of target keywords and authoritative mentions.
  5. Monitor SERP movement and competitor content positioning through SEO tools.
    Pitfalls & Limits:
  • Overusing press releases or flooding the web with low-quality content can lead to diminishing returns or potential penalties.
  • Cheap or low-quality distribution channels may have limited reach or credibility.
  • This tactic may not work well for queries requiring deep expertise or fact-checked content, as press releases lack editorial oversight.
    Example: Max Rosalikov used a weekend press release to push a negative comparison post from #7 to #11 on fatgrid.com’s branded query, successfully demoting the negative content off page one.

Implementation Checklist

  3. Publish the press release, ensuring inclusion of target keywords and authoritative mentions.
  4. Monitor SERP movement and competitor content positioning through SEO tools.
