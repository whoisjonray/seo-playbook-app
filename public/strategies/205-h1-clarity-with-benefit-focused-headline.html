Full Strategy Details

  1. H1 Clarity with Benefit-Focused Headline
  • What It Is: Use a short, clear, simple H1 headline conveying the main benefit or what the product/service is, avoiding jargon or overly clever language.
    • Why It Works: Visitors look for immediate clarity and relevance; confusing or technical language leads to disengagement and bounce. Also aids SEO by aligning H1 with search intent/keywords on SEO pages.
    • When To Use: All landing pages, especially those not purely SEO driven; product, service, or campaign landing pages.
