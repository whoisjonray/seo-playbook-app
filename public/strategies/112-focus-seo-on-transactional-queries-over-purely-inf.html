Focus SEO on Transactional Queries Over Purely Informational Queries

Full Strategy Details

Focus SEO on Transactional Queries Over Purely Informational Queries

What It Is: Target keywords with clear intent to purchase or take action rather than just informational queries, especially important as AI-generated featured snippets reduce clicks on informational results.
Why It Works: AI overviews or featured snippets answer informational queries directly on SERPs, cutting click-through rates (by about 34.5%). Transactional queries still drive meaningful clicks that convert.
When To Use: Ecommerce, lead generation, service-driven sites focusing on conversion-oriented content.
Execution Steps:

  1. Audit keywords by intent type: separate transactional from informational.
  2. Optimize content targeting transactional queries by emphasizing brand value, product benefits, and calls to action.
  3. For informational queries, add value by explaining why your brand/product is superior.
  4. Track click-through rates and adjust to focus more on transactional content where you win.
    Pitfalls & Limits: Purely informational content may suffer reduced clicks due to AI snippets on SERPs; avoid relying solely on informational content for conversion.
    Example: Google’s Gary Ilas confirmed AI overviews impact clicks mostly on informational queries; thus, focusing SEO on transactional content is more effective.
