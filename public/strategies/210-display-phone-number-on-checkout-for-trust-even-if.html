Full Strategy Details

Display Phone Number on Checkout for Trust (Even if Calls Are Rare)

  • What It Is: Place a visible phone number on checkout or payment pages to reassure customers they can reach support if needed.
    • Why It Works: Presence of phone indicates credibility and customer service availability, which increases buyer confidence and reduces friction, even if actual calls are few.
    • When To Use: Ecommerce stores and service sites during checkout or conversion stages where trust signals help final purchase decisions.
    • Execution Steps:
      1. Add phone number visibly in checkout footer or near payment submission button.
      2. Use a staffed support number if possible; if not, provide a monitored voicemail/phone line.
      3. Optionally explain purpose (e.g., “Call us if you have any questions before ordering”).
      4. Monitor call volume and impact on conversion.
    • Pitfalls & Limits: Fake or unmonitored numbers damage trust. Expect minimal actual calls, but don’t eliminate the phone presence.
    • Example: Sites with phone number displayed had trust signals improved without increase in disruptive calls.
