Full Strategy Details

Author Pages to Enhance E-A-T and Rank for Author Names

What It Is

Create dedicated author directory pages listing an author’s content and providing detailed author bios to capture searches for author names and boost expertise signals.

Why It Works

Builds author credibility, aligns with Google’s E-A-T focus by emphasizing expertise and trustworthiness, and improves chances for author name-related traffic.

When To Use

Content-heavy sites, blogs, news publishers, sites with multiple authors contributing regularly.

Execution Steps

  1. For each author, make a dedicated author page with a well-written bio describing their credentials, area of expertise, and contributions.
  2. List all articles or content pieces by the author on that page.
  3. Internally link from articles to author pages and vice versa.
  4. Optimize author pages for author name keywords.

Pitfalls & Limits

Thin or poorly written bios miss opportunity to build trust; if authors are not known or authoritative, may not add much SEO value; duplicative content across author pages can dilute signals.

Implementation Checklist

  1. For each author, make a dedicated author page with a well written bio describing their credentials, area of expertise, and contributions. List all articles or content pieces by the author on that page.
  2. Internally link from articles to author pages and vice versa.
  3. Optimize author pages for author name keywords.
