Full Strategy Details

Leverage Press Releases for Brand Trust and AI Search Signals

-

What It Is

Distribute press releases highlighting your brand’s trustworthiness, specialization, and notable achievements to widely-cited platforms that are scraped and synthesized by AI search engines and aggregators.

Why It Works

Press releases serve as authoritative, citable sources that AI models and overviews use to generate responses, boosting perceived brand credibility and visibility in AI responses beyond traditional SEO benefits.

When To Use

Especially effective for brands looking to enhance their AI search presence quickly, companies launching new products, or seeking to outrank competitors on brand reputation.

Execution Steps

 1. Write comprehensive press releases focusing on your brand’s strengths, specialization, and testimonials.  
 2. Use affordable press release distribution services (e.g., AB Newswire's bulk packages or Fiverr options).  
 3. Avoid or carefully consider linking directly to your site to prevent associating with low-quality backlinks.  
 4. Track mentions and AI-generated results for brand name queries to assess impact.  
 5. Repurpose press release content on your website in structured and detailed brand information pages.

-

Pitfalls & Limits

 - Avoid sending to unreliable or spammy press release services.  
 - Linking strategy must avoid harmful backlink associations.  
 - Overuse may cause diminishing returns, especially if content is low value or repetitive.
