Full Strategy Details

Post-Purchase Survey with Incentive

-

What It Is

Send a detailed post-purchase survey (7-8 questions) within 48 hours of purchase, offering a gift card to encourage responses.

Why It Works

Incentivizes customers to provide rich data on their decision-making process, competitor comparisons, and desired improvements, while increasing repeat purchases (4-5x more valuable customers).

When To Use

Ecommerce stores with repeat buyers, product-agnostic (works for low or high price products), helpful for gathering depth of buyer insights and boosting customer retention.

Execution Steps

 1. Create a multi-question post-purchase survey exploring buyer motivators, competitor research, and improvement ideas.  
 2. Offer a gift card or credit redeemable on the site if completed within 48 hours.  
 3. Automate survey delivery via email post-purchase.  
 4. Use collected data to refine marketing messaging and product offers.  
 5. Track survey respondents’ repeat purchase behavior for performance impact.

-

Pitfalls & Limits

Incentive cost must be balanced with expected value; low price products might have lower margin for gift card offers. Survey fatigue if done too often.

Implementation Checklist

  1. Create a multi question post purchase survey exploring buyer motivators, competitor research, and improvement ideas. Offer a gift card or credit redeemable on the site if completed within 48 hours.
  2. Automate survey delivery via email post purchase. Use collected data to refine marketing messaging and product offers.
  3. Track survey respondents’ repeat purchase behavior for performance impact.
