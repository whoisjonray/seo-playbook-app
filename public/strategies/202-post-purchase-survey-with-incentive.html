Post-Purchase Survey with Incentive

Category: Advanced Tactics

Metadata

  • Type: Advanced Tactics
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • Time to Impact: Hours to Days
  • Required Tools: None specified

Quick Summary

Send a detailed post-purchase survey (7-8 questions) within 48 hours of purchase, offering a gift card to encourage responses.

Full Strategy Details

  1. Post-Purchase Survey with Incentive
  • What It Is: Send a detailed post-purchase survey (7-8 questions) within 48 hours of purchase, offering a gift card to encourage responses.
    • Why It Works: Incentivizes customers to provide rich data on their decision-making process, competitor comparisons, and desired improvements, while increasing repeat purchases (4-5x more valuable customers).
    • When To Use: Ecommerce stores with repeat buyers, product-agnostic (works for low or high price products), helpful for gathering depth of buyer insights and boosting customer retention.
    • Execution Steps:
      1. Create a multi-question post-purchase survey exploring buyer motivators, competitor research, and improvement ideas.
      2. Offer a gift card or credit redeemable on the site if completed within 48 hours.
      3. Automate survey delivery via email post-purchase.
      4. Use collected data to refine marketing messaging and product offers.
      5. Track survey respondents‚Äô repeat purchase behavior for performance impact.
    • Pitfalls & Limits: Incentive cost must be balanced with expected value; low price products might have lower margin for gift card offers. Survey fatigue if done too often.
    • Example: Deployed on 300+ stores, from $50 to $3,000 average order values, customers who complete survey buy faster and become 4-5x more valuable.
