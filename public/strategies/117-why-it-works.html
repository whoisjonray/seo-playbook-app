Multiple H1 Tags Strategy

Category: Advanced Tactics

Metadata

  • Type: Advanced Tactics
  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • Time to Impact: 1-3 Months
  • Required Tools: None specified

Full Strategy Details

Why It Works:

Google can interpret multiple H1s as structuring dual but related topics, improving relevancy signals; it challenges the old “one H1 per page” best practice, reflecting evolving SEO nuances.

When To Use

Large, content-rich websites where pages cover closely related topics or when split testing demonstrates benefits; sites with complex content architecture.

Execution Steps

  • Create a second textual H1 header following the first, both relevant to the content.
  • Ensure no logos or images are marked as H1s to avoid confusion.
  • Implement on a test group of pages, leaving others with only one H1.
  • Monitor rankings, traffic, and user engagement over time.

Pitfalls & Limits

Avoid adding second H1 with irrelevant content; blindly adding multiple H1s without testing can harm SEO; not guaranteed universally effective—results may vary with site and niche.

Example

At TripAdvisor and other sites, pages with two relevant H1s repeatedly outperformed those with one H1. Removing the second H1 decreased sessions, confirming the benefit.

  1. Monitor rankings, traffic, and user engagement over time.
