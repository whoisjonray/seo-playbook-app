Full Strategy Details

Including Keywords in URL Slugs for Ranking Benefit

What It Is

Using descriptive, keyword-rich words in page URL slugs rather than overly short or generic ones.

Why It Works

Keywords in URLs give Google and users explicit context about page content and improve relevance signals.

When To Use

All website pages, especially service and product pages targeting specific keywords.

Execution Steps

- Review existing URL slugs on your site.  
- Add relevant keywords naturally into the slug without keyword stuffing.  
- Avoid overly short URLs that omit target keywords.  
- Maintain simple URL structure without unnecessary folders if possible.

Pitfalls & Limits

Avoid creating extremely long URLs or using poor keyword stuffing; changing URLs on live sites requires proper redirects.
