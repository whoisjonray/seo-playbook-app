Avoid Shady Link Building Methods Despite Effectiveness

What It Is

Recognizing that link schemes like private blog networks (PBNs), expired domains, guest posts on low-quality sites, and paid homepage links are effective short term but carry significant risks.

Why It Works

These tactics can manipulate link metrics and temporarily boost rankings, but violate Google guidelines and risk penalties. They may pass link juice but increase manual action likelihood.

When To Use

Generally avoid; only with full risk understanding in aggressive SEO campaigns with contingency plans.

Execution Steps

  • Identify shady tactics in your link building and eliminate or minimize them.
  • Instead, focus on natural, high-quality backlinks.
  • Regularly audit backlink profile for spammy links and remove if needed (carefully).

Pitfalls & Limits

Using these risky tactics can cause ranking drops, penalties, and loss of trust. Recovery is costly and uncertain.
