Full Strategy Details

Use of Exact Match Anchor Text in Guest Post Links Sparingly

What It Is: Including exact match keyword anchors in backlinks within guest posts moderately to improve targeted keyword rankings.
Why It Works: Exact match anchors send explicit signals to Google about the topic of the linked page; limited use avoids spam triggers.
When To Use: During natural guest posting campaigns where links are few and high quality.
Execution Steps:

  • Write guest posts that include links with natural, relevant exact match anchors to your target keyword pages.
  • Limit the total number of exact match anchor links to a handful (around 5-10) to avoid over-optimization.
  • Ensure guest post content is valuable and editorially sound.
    Pitfalls & Limits: Excessive exact match link anchors trigger spam penalties (common with mass guest post campaigns).
    Example: Client with ~400 guest posts over 2 years, rich in keyword anchors, got hit by algorithm updates penalizing those links.
