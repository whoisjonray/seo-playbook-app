Full Strategy Details

Include Brand Name in Page Titles

What It Is: Append the brand name to the page title using a separator (e.g., “|” or “-”) at the end of the title tag.
Why It Works: Adding the brand name improves brand recognition, contributes to trust and click-through rates, and helps associate content with the website’s authority in search results.
When To Use: Publisher sites, ecommerce, local businesses, and any site wanting stronger brand presence in SERPs.
Execution Steps:

  1. Adjust the title tag template or CMS to append the brand name at the end of titles.
  2. Use a vertical bar or dash as a separator.
  3. Ensure title length remains within 50-60 characters for optimal display.
    Pitfalls & Limits:
  • Overly long titles can get truncated in SERPs if brand name addition is not optimized.
  • Less critical for generic informational pages with highly competitive keywords.
    Example: The New York Post currently does not do this but it is recommended as a best practice.
