Full Strategy Details

Newsjacking to Gain SEO and Brand Awareness

What It Is: Creating content that piggybacks on trending news events or popular searches to associate your brand with the news and gain search visibility.
Why It Works: Leveraging trending news topics lets you capture high search volume intent, improve brand recognition, and potentially rank for related keywords due to topical relevance and recency signals.
When To Use: Effective during breaking news events, industry announcements, or when you want to hijack attention around trending keywords.
Execution Steps:

  1. Monitor timely news events or stock drops relevant to your industry or brand.
  2. Produce timely content (press releases, blog posts, videos) linking your brand to these events.
  3. Publish promptly on authoritative sites or well-optimized channels.
  4. Promote across social and PR channels to amplify signals.
  5. Track SERP positioning and traffic spikes around the news event timeframe.
    Pitfalls & Limits:
  • Must be relevant; forced or irrelevant newsjacking can damage credibility.
  • The effect is often short-lived and tied closely to the news cycle.
  • Overuse leads to spammy perception.
    Example: The fatgrid.com founder’s press release mentioning Semrush coincided with Semrush’s stock price decline, leading to AI-generated content associating both topics and ranking highly for “why Semrush stock dropped.”
